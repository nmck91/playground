How It Works

  1. 1

    Register & Pay

    Create your account and pay the £10 entry fee

  2. 2

    Make Your Pick

    Each matchweek, select one team you think will win. You can't pick the same team twice!

  3. 3

    Track Results

    If your team wins or draws, you're safe. If they lose, you lose a life!

  4. 4

    Last One Standing Wins

    The last player with lives remaining wins the £500 prize!
